## Scoring Philosophy

**Score as if a senior SEO consultant is reviewing this article for a paying client.**

- Lenient scoring produces mediocre articles that never rank
- "Close enough" does not pass. Only "certifiably excellent" passes
- When in doubt, reject. The cost of publishing a weak article exceeds the cost of revision
- A single CRITICAL issue forces rejection regardless of total score

### Instant Zero Rules (any one = 0 points for that category)

- Title missing target keyword → Title score = 0
- Multiple h1 tags or no h1 → Heading score = 0
- Zero source URLs cited → Citation score = 0
- Word count below 70% of competitor average → Word count = 0
- Brief outline completely ignored → Brief compliance = 0
- 3+ AI-sounding phrases ("It's important to note", "It's worth mentioning", em dashes "—") → Content quality -15 points

## SEO Scoring (100 points)

### Title & Meta (15 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Title length | 5 | 30-55 chars (no mobile truncation) | 56-60: -2 / 61+: 0 |
| KW position | 5 | KW within **first 20 chars** | 21-30: -2 / 30+: -4 / Missing: 0 |
| Meta description | 5 | 120-155 chars, KW included, specific benefit, CTA | No KW: -2 / 160+: -3 / Missing: 0 |

**Title bonus deductions:**
- No number (when applicable) → -1
- No visual markers (brackets, quotes) → -1
- Too similar to competitor titles → -3

### Heading Structure (20 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| h1 uniqueness | 3 | Exactly one h1, matches title | Multiple/missing: 0 |
| Hierarchy | 5 | No skips, logical nesting | h2→h4 skip: 0 / Logic error: -3 |
| KW in headings | 5 | **60%+** of h2s contain KW/related terms | 50-59%: -2 / <50%: -4 |
| Heading appeal | 4 | Compelling: questions, numbers, contrast, benefits | Bureaucratic ("About X", "Overview of X"): -2 each |
| Heading count | 3 | Within ±10% of competitor average | ±11-20%: -1 / ±20%+: -2 |

### Content Quality (30 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Word count | 5 | **110%+** of competitor average | 100-109%: -2 / 90-99%: -3 / 70-89%: -4 / <70%: 0 |
| KW density | 5 | **1.5-2.5%** in natural context | 1-1.4%: -2 / 2.6-3%: -1 / >3%: -3 / <1%: -4 |
| Related KW coverage | 5 | **80%+** of brief's keyword list | 70-79%: -2 / 60-69%: -3 / <60%: -4 |
| Internal links | 5 | **5-10** with KW-rich anchor text | 3-4: -2 / 1-2: -3 / 0: 0 |
| Citations | 5 | **15+ sources**, inline (name+date+URL), 50%+ primary | 10-14: -2 / 5-9: -3 / <5: 0 / 50%+ missing URLs: -3 |
| CTA placement | 5 | 2-4 locations, perfectly contextual, zero pushiness | Abrupt CTA: -2 each / 0 CTAs: -3 / 5+: -2 |

**Content bonus deductions (up to -15):**
- AI phrasing → -3 per instance
- Same sentence ending 3+ times in a row → -2 per instance
- "Furthermore"/"Additionally"/"Moreover" 5+ times → -3
- Bullet points >50% of body → -5
- Data cited without "so what" connection → -2 per instance
- Generic opening (no hook in first 3 lines) → -5

### Technical SEO (10 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Image alt | 3 | All images: KW-containing, descriptive alt | Missing: 0 / Generic ("image1"): -2 |
| URL structure | 2 | English slug, KW, ≤3 levels, hyphens | Non-English: -1 / 4+ levels: -1 |
| Schema | 5 | **Full compliance** with brief's Schema design | Partial: -2 / None: 0 / HowTo recommended: -5 |

### Brief Compliance (25 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Required topics | 10 | **100%** of brief topics covered | 1 missing: -3 / 2: -6 / 3+: 0 |
| Differentiation | 8 | **3+** unique elements implemented | 2: -3 / 1: -5 / 0: 0 |
| User memo | 7 | **All** specified target/tone/CTA reflected | Partial: -3 / Ignored: 0 |

## AIO/GEO Scoring (100 points)

### Citability (30 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Summary passages | 12 | **All h2s** have 134-167 word self-contained summary blocks | 1 missing: -3 / <50%: 0 |
| Direct Answer | 10 | Opens with **specific** 1-2 sentence answer then expands. Clear definitions | Vague intro ("Let's look at..."): -5 / Missing: 0 |
| Definition+Number+Source triad | 8 | **All major sections** have the triad | >50%: -3 / <50%: -6 |

### Structural Readability (20 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Heading clarity | 7 | AI can parse structure accurately. Headings alone tell the story | Ambiguous headings: -2 each |
| Lists & tables | 7 | Comparisons **always** in tables, procedures **always** numbered, 3+ items **always** listed | Table-worthy data in prose: -3 / List-worthy items in prose: -2 |
| Passage independence | 6 | Each section **stands alone**. No prior reading required | 3+ back-references ("as mentioned above"): -3 |

### Multi-Modal (15 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Image alt quality | 5 | **50+ char** descriptive alt that AI can understand | Generic: -3 / Missing: 0 |
| Diagrams & tables | 5 | **2+** tables or diagram instructions | 1: -2 / 0: -4 |
| Structured data | 5 | Schema supports AI citation. Article + BreadcrumbList + Organization minimum 3 types | 2: -2 / ≤1: -4 |

### Authority Signals (20 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Source authority | 8 | Primary sources (govt/academic/official) **50%+** of all citations | 30-49%: -3 / <30%: -5 |
| Author info | 6 | Author credentials/experience **specifically** stated in body | "Expert" without specifics: -3 / No author info: -5 |
| Brand credibility | 6 | Company expertise shown **with data** | Mention only (no data): -3 / No mention: -5 |

### Technical AIO (15 pts)

| Check | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| FAQ schema candidates | 5 | **3+** Q&A sections that are Schema-ready | 1-2: -2 / 0: -3 |
| Passage optimization | 5 | 134-167 word blocks in **all** major sections | >50%: -2 / <50%: -4 |
| Meta completeness | 5 | Published date, updated date, author name, author profile link **all** present | 1 missing: -1 / 2+ missing: -3 |

## E-E-A-T Scoring (100 points)

| Element | Points | Full marks | Deductions |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Experience** | 30 | **2+** specific first-hand experiences with dates, places, numbers. "After implementing X, Y improved by Z%" level of specificity | Abstract experience only: -10 / 1 instance only: -8 / None: 0 |
| **Expertise** | 25 | Industry terminology used **accurately**. Analysis goes beyond surface to "why" mechanisms. Content a layperson couldn't write | Terminology errors: -5 each / Wikipedia-level only: -10 |
| **Authoritativeness** | 20 | **10+** authoritative source citations. Specific author credentials mentioned. Industry body/academic references | 5-9 sources: -5 / <5: -10 / No credentials: -5 |
| **Trustworthiness** | 25 | All data sourced. Update date shown. Bias disclosed. Numbers verified. Counterarguments and limitations fairly presented | Unsourced data: -3 each / One-sided promotion (no counterarguments): -8 / Missing limitations: -5 |

## Scoring Formula

```
Total = SEO × 0.30 + AIO × 0.25 + E-E-A-T × 0.25 + Brief Compliance (within SEO) × 0.20
```

| Total Score | Verdict | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **90+** | **Approved** | Proceed to Phase 4. This article deserves to rank |
| **80-89** | **Conditional** | Approve with HIGH improvement notes. Minor revision requested |
| **≤79** | **Rejected** | CRITICAL improvement instructions sent to Writer. Rewrite required |

**Judgment principles:**
- When in doubt, reject
- Any CRITICAL issue = rejection regardless of total score
- "Almost good" doesn't pass. Only "certifiably excellent" passes
